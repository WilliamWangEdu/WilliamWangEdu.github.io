Our Services 服务内容

We offer end-to-end guidance for international students seeking both undergraduate and graduate admissions. All services are customized based on your academic profile, interests, and long-term goals. 我们为本科与研究生申请者提供从头到尾的升学指导服务，所有内容将根据您的背景、兴趣和长期目标量身定制。

  • 🎯 Personalized Consultation:个性化咨询： One-on-one sessions to understand your background, preferences, and goals — whether academic fit or long-term career potential. 一对一咨询以了解您的背景、偏好与目标，帮助您选择适合的学术方向与职业路径。
  • 🏫 School Selection Strategy:选校策略： Tailored recommendations for schools in the U.S., Canada, U.K., or Australia. 根据个人情况推荐适合的美国、加拿大、英国或澳洲高校。
  • 📝 Essay & SOP Review:文书修改： Support on structuring, writing, and refining your personal statement or SOP. 协助撰写和修改个人陈述与动机信，突出您的独特优势。
  • 🎤 Mock Admissions Interviews:模拟面试： Simulated interviews with personalized feedback. 提供真实还原的模拟面试及改进建议。
  • 📋 Application Planning:申请规划： Help organizing deadlines, materials, and overall timeline. 协助梳理申请时间线、材料准备等关键事项。
  • 🛂 Visa Support:签证支持： Advice on financial docs, interview prep, and visa navigation. 提供签证准备建议，包括资金证明与面试指导。
  • 📚 Post-Admission Tutoring:入学后辅导： Help with economics, analytics, finance, or Excel/R/Python coursework. 提供经济学、金融、数据分析、Excel/R/Python等课程辅导。
  • ✈️ Pre-Departure Orientation:行前指导： Packing lists, budgeting tips, insurance advice, and cultural preparation. 行前准备建议，包括行李清单、保险、预算和文化适应等内容。
